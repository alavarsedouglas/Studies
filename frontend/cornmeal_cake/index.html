Cornmeal Cake With Guava Paste

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 glass (American) of milk
  • 1 cup (American) of cornmeal
  • 1 cup (American) of sugar
  • 1/2 cup (American) oil
  • 1 cup (American) wheat flour
  • 1 spoon of baking powder
  • guava and chunks
  • fennel to taste
Buy Ingredients

How To Do

  1. Mix the egg, milk, oil, sugar, cornmeal and mix in a blender.
  2. Pour the mixture into a bowl, and mix with the flour and baking powder.
  3. Pour the dough into a greased mold with butter and flour, and add a layer of guava paste. Repeat this process until you fill the entire shape.
  4. Take to preheated medium oven (180Â°C) for 40 minutes.
